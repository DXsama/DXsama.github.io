Z变换的导出

\(抽样信号的拉式变换\rightarrow离散信号的Z变换\) \(信号的理想抽样：\) \[\begin{align*} x_s(t) & =x(t)\cdot\delta_T(t) \\ & = x(t)\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}\delta(t-nT) \\ & =\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}x(nT)\delta(t-nT) \\ \end{align*}\]

\(对x_s(t)取拉氏变换：\) \[\begin{align*} X_s(s) & =\mathscr{L}[x_s(t)]=\mathscr{L}\left[\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}x(nT)\delta(t-nT)\right] \\ & =\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}x(nT)\mathscr{L}[\delta(t-nT)] \\ & =\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}x(nT)e^{-snT} \end{align*}\]

\(引入复变量：z=e^{sT}，令T=1，x(nT)=x(n)：\) \[\left.X_s(s)\right|_{z=e^{sT}}=\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}x(n)z^{-n}=X(z)\]

\(对任一信号x(n)的（双边）z变换式为：\) \[X(z)=\sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}x(n)z^{-n}\]

典型序列的Z变换

序列\(x(n)\) Z变换\((\lvert z\rvert>\lvert a\rvert)\)
\[\delta(n)\] \[1\qquad \lvert z\rvert<\infty\]
\[u(n)\] \[\frac{z}{z-1}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>1\]
\[-u(-n-1)\] \[\frac{z}{z-1}\qquad \lvert z\rvert<1\]
\[nu(n)\] \[\frac{z}{(z-1)^2}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>1\]
\[a^nu(n)\] \[\frac{z}{z-a}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>\lvert a\rvert\]
\[\sin(n\omega_0)u(n)\] \[\frac{z\sin\omega_0}{z^2-2z\cos\omega_0+1}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>1\]
\[\cos(n\omega_0)u(n)\] \[\frac{z(z-\cos\omega_0)}{z^2-2z\cos\omega_0+1}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>1\]
\[\beta^n\sin(n\omega_0)u(n)\] \[\frac{\beta z\sin\omega_0}{z^2-2\beta z\cos\omega_0+\beta^2}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>\beta\]
\[\beta^n\cos(n\omega_0)u(n)\] \[\frac{z(z-\beta\cos\omega_0)}{z^2-2\beta z\cos\omega_0+\beta^2}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>\beta\]
\[\sinh(n\omega_0)u(n)\] \[\frac{z\sinh\omega_0}{z^2-2z\cosh\omega_0+1}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>\max{(\lvert e^{\omega_0}\rvert,\lvert e^{-\omega_0}\rvert)}\]
\[\cosh(n\omega_0)u(n)\] \[\frac{z(z-\cosh\omega_0)}{z^2-2z\cosh\omega_0+1}\qquad \lvert z\rvert>\max{(\lvert e^{\omega_0}\rvert,\lvert e^{-\omega_0}\rvert)}\]

Z变换的性质

性质 序列\(x(n)\) Z变换\(X(z)\)
\[线性性质\] \[ax_1(n)+bx_2(n)\] \[aX_1(z)+bX_2(z)\]
\[位移性质（双边z变换）\] \[x(n\pm m)\] \[z^{\pm m}X(z)\]
\[位移性质（单边z变换）\] \[x(n+m)u(n)\] \[x(n-m)u(n)\] \[z^m[X(z)-\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}x(k)z^{-k}]\]\[z^{-m}[X(z)+\sum_{k=-m}^{-1}x(k)z^{-k}]\]
\[序列线性加权（z域微分）\] \[n^mx(n)\] \[\left[-z\frac{d}{dz}\right]^mX(z)\]
\[序列指数加权（z域尺度变换）\] \[a^nx(n)\] \[X\left(\frac{z}{a}\right)\quad (R_{x1}<\lvert\frac{z}{a}\rvert<R_{x2})\]
\[z域积分\] \[\frac{x(n)}{n+m}\] \[z^m\int_{z}^{\infty}k^{-(m+1)}X(k)dk\]
\[时域卷积定理\] \[x(n)\ast h(n)\] \[X(z)H(z)\quad\max(R_{x1},R_{h1})<\lvert z\rvert<\min(R_{x2},R_{h2})\]
  • \(序列相乘（z域卷积定理）：\)

\[\begin{align*} \mathscr{Z}[x(n)h(n)] & =\frac{1}{2\pi j}\oint_{C_1}X\left(\frac{z}{v}\right)H(v)v^{-1}dv\quad \\ 或 \\ & =\frac{1}{2\pi j}\oint_{C_2}X(v)H\left(\frac{z}{v}\right)v^{-1}dv \end{align*}\]

\(其中C_1，C_2分别为X\left(\frac{z}{v}\right)与H(v)或X(v)与H\left(\frac{z}{v}\right)收敛域重叠部分内逆时针旋转的围线\) \(收敛域：R_{x1}R_{h1}<|z|<R_{x2}R_{h2}\)

  • \(初值定理\) \(若在n<M时，x(n)=0，即x(n)为右边序列时，有：\)

    \[\begin{align*} x(M) & =\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}z^MX(z) \\ x(M+1) & =\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}[z^{M+1}X(z)-zx(M)] \\ x(M+2) & =\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}[z^{M+2}X(z)-z^2x(M)-zx(M+1)] \\ \end{align*}\]

    \(若x(t)为因果序列，即M=0时：\)

    \[\begin{align*} x(0) & =\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}X(z) \\ x(1) & =\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}[zF(z)-zf(0)] \\ x(2) & =\lim_{z\rightarrow\infty}[z^2F(z)-z^2f(0)-zf(1)] \\ \end{align*}\]

  • \(终值定理\) \[若x(n)是右边序列，x(\infty)=\lim_{z\rightarrow1}[(z-1)X(z)]\] \(条件：X(z)的极点必须在单位圆内（在单位圆上只能位于z=\pm1且是一阶极点）\)
